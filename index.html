Zlatan Ibrahimovic

Over the past few years soccer fans have witnessed a young Swedish soccer player grow into one of the most talented players of the generation. His arrogance has won the hearts of millions of soccer fans world-wide. Although he has not won any Balon de Oro, he is still one of the most appreciated players in the game. He is one of the few players who has not let his destitution stop him from acheiving his goals. 15 years later, we still know him as Zlatan Ibrahimovic.

The Beginning

Ibrahimović was born in Sweden to a Muslim father, Šefik Ibrahimović, who moved to Sweden in 1977, and a Croatian Catholic mother, Jurka Gravić, who had also moved to Sweden. Zlatan grew up in a poor neighborhood where he would recieve his first pair of soccer boots at the age of six. His soccer skills gained him popularity in his neighborhood. Though he was excelling in his soccer club, at the age of 15, he was close to quitting his football career, and work at the docks nearby. Thankfully, his soccer manager convinced him to stay.
